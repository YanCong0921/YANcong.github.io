A tree 住宅

室内 住宅 试验 0000-00-00

该项目是2020年学校的毕业设计作品，以重庆涪陵区的一处老房为蓝本，经过设计改造，探索设计与老建筑之间的关系，通过设计使老建筑焕发新生。大多数的老房子光线都特别昏暗，因此在本案中设计特别强调了阳光 光线对建筑的影响，算是一个试验作品。

This project is the graduation design work of the school in 2020. Based on an old house in Fuling District, Chongqing, it explores the relationship between design and old buildings through design and transformation, and makes the old buildings rejuvenate through design. Most of the old houses are particularly dim, so the design in this case particularly emphasizes the impact of sunlight on the building, which is regarded as an experimental work.

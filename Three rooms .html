建筑-室内 公共 试验 0000-00-00

该项目是2020年学校的比赛作品，项目构想的是北方窑洞的地貌特征，是一次将建筑嵌入地块中的尝试。设计以简单的山字头图案为主，通体建筑以白色为主。意在用简单的造型和大片面的白色构造一个干净整体的建筑。同时公共区域的景观小品也参考了阳光对建筑的印象，算是对建筑光线应用的一次尝试。

This project is the competition work of the school in 2020. The project is conceived as the geomorphic features of the northern cave dwellings, and is an attempt to embed the building into the plot. The design is based on simple mountain letterhead pattern, and the whole building is mainly white. It is intended to construct a clean and integral building with simple shapes and large areas of white. At the same time, the landscape sketch in the public area also refers to the impression of the sun on the building, which is an attempt to apply the building light.

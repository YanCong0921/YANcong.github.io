YANCong

永缘锦庭销售中心

室内 商业 重庆 0000-00-00

该项目位于重庆沙坪坝双碑的永缘锦庭楼盘，原址位于多栋居民楼的地下空间。建筑遗留的承重柱错综复杂，且体量巨大。为了弱化承重柱对空间的影响，本案采用了曲线设计，利用曲线墙体包裹承重柱消除空间的割裂感。设计风格上偏向诧寂风，意在表达一个古朴有质感的空间。

The project is located in the Yongyuan Jinting building in Shuangbei, Shapingba, Chongqing. The original site is located in the underground space of several residential buildings. The load-bearing columns left by the building are complex and massive. In order to weaken the impact of load-bearing column on space, this project adopts curve design, and uses curve wall to wrap the load-bearing column to eliminate the sense of space fragmentation. The design style tends to surprise the quiet wind, which is intended to express an ancient and simple space with texture.
